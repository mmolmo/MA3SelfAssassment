My Journey at UCF

About Me

Hi there, my name is James Li and I am currently a student at the University of Central Florida. I am pursuing a degree in web design and am excited about the endless possibilities that this field has to offer.

How I Got Here

I applied to UCF in the summer of 2019 as a high school senior after looking through many extensive college options. I first applied to Valencia College and then to UCF. When I got an email back from UCF, I accepted the decision as I liked the overall environment, campus, and other features. I chose to apply for the current major as web design is something I would like to get into and something that I feel is still going to be accessible even in the upcoming years.

Year 1

I attended a virtual orientation, took and finished general education classes at the main campus and started on some lower-level digital courses.

Year 2

For year 2, I took and learned more content about low-level and mid-level digital courses. I was able to gain some more knowledge in the field and refine some experiences with the software.

Future Plans

As I move forward in my education at UCF, I plan to continue learning and growing in the web design field. I am excited to take on more challenging projects and gain experience through internships or other related opportunities. My ultimate goal is to use my skills to help create meaningful and impactful digital experiences for users.
